make the most of your professional keepsake

attire

- Wear clothes that you feel comfortable in,
this helps you exude confidence in your photo!
- Try to ensure everyone does not wear exact
matching clothes unless that is the theme of the photos!
- Consider the location of your session! Dressing for
your location can help make your photos feel more natural.

bring things

Feel free to bring any props with you that could
bring your passions and personality into the photo
(musical instruments, special jewelry, etc.)!

We can discuss locations
and include everything important to you.
